NOTIFICATIONS:
1. An HTML document that describes frame layout (called a frameset document ) has a different makeup than an HTML document without frames.

2. A standard document has one HEAD section and one BODY . A frameset document has a HEAD , and a FRAMESET in place of the BODY .

3. Targeting multiple frames at once Multiple frames can only be updated at once if they are in the same row or column of a "super" frameset.
